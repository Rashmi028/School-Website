SCHOOL PRAYER


From the crown of my head to the soles of my feet;
I give to you Lord my being complete,
For guidance and wisdom throughout this day,
Every step that I take, very word that I say,
Cause me to meet the ones I should see,
Help me to speak the words that I say.
Keep me from folks whoever they be;
Unless I can help them or they can help me.
Help me treat others as I'd had treat me.
And as I forgive others dear Lord forgive me.
Temper each word and act with the love.
And give me the wisdom that comes from above.
I thank thee dear God for life, home and love.
For food,raiment and loving Parents' gifts from above.
For your guidance and grace to save me from sin
For life everlasting through your Holy name.
